Celebrating Afghan Heritage Through Dress

We preserve the art of Afghan dressmaking and empower communities through creativity, identity, and design.

Our Mission

Zahra Clothing Designs is a nonprofit initiative that celebrates traditional Afghan dresses as a form of art, resistance, and identity. We provide education, inspiration, and support for Afghan women and artists.
